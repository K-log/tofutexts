Prolog

  • Declaritive

  • Recursion(no "for" or "while" loops)

  • Relations (no functions)

  • Unification

    • With the help of variables
    • Very powerful

  • Syntax

    • Atoms
      • An atom can be one of two things
      • A string of characters made up of upper and lower-case letters, digits, underscores, and must begin with a lower-case character.
      • An arbitrary sequence of characters enclosed in single quotes.
      • A string of special characters
    • Variables
      • A string of upper-case or lower-case letters, digits and underscores. Starts with either an upper-case letter or an underscore.
