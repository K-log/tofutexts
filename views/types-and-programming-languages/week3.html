The pairs (true --> true) and (false --> false) is not in the (one step) reduction relation. Why?
Because it's reflexive.

UBE reduction vs Beta reduction in lambda calculus
  • The UBE reduction is an "evaluation strategy". The rules restrict what you can use as a redex at each step.
  • The UBE reduction must match the entire term not some subterm. The lambda calculus beta reduction allows you to search for a redux anywhere in a term.
